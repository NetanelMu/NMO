About Us

At NMO, we specialize in delivering advanced software solutions and expert remote support for building automation and control systems. From diagnosing faults to deploying upgrades and fine-tuning performance, our team is available 24/7 to ensure your systems stay efficient, secure, and always online. With a strong focus on reliability, speed, and personal service, NMO is your trusted partner for keeping infrastructure running — anytime, anywhere.

What we do?

  • Troubleshooting & Fault Resolution Fast and accurate diagnosis of system issues — with remote or on-site solutions that minimize downtime.

  • 24/7 Remote & Phone Support Our experts are always a call or click away, ready to assist with any issue, big or small

  • Consulting & System Planning Whether you're planning a new system or improving an existing one, we provide smart guidance based on years of hands-on experience.
